About Us

Welcome to OdinaTechCNG we are driving the future of transportation by making clean, affordable energy accessible to everyone. We specialize in converting standard petrol and diseal vehicles (generators) into highly efficient, duel-fuel Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) systems. With fuel prices steadily rising, our mission is simple: to help every day drivers,fleet managers, commercial transporters and businesses cut their fuel consumptionup by 50-60% while lowering their carbon footprint .


Why Choose us

  • Massive cost savings
  • Fewer oil changes
  • longer life span for spark plugs and pipes
  • lower overall maintenance cost over time
  • Almost zero particulate matter(that nasty black smoke)
  • The cylinders used to store CNG are built from high strength composite materials or thick steel, designed to survive severe crashes and extreeme heat
  • duel-fuel advantages that enables simless transmission between petrol and CNG
